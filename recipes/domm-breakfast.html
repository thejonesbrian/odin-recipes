Dutch Oven Mountain Men Breakfast

Description

The Mountain Man Breakfast is a hearty, all-in-one breakfast skillet packed with sausage, hash browns, eggs, and cheese. Traditionally cooked in a Dutch oven over a campfire, it's ideal for camping trips or anyone craving a filling, protein-packed morning meal.

This breakfast is sure to satisfy even the hungriest adventurer and can easily feed a crowd. It's rustic, flavorful, and the perfect way to start a long day of work or outdoor activity.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pork sausage
  • 1 (2 pound) package frozen hash brown potatoes
  • 12 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter or oil for greasing

Steps

  1. Preheat your Dutch oven over medium heat or a campfire.
  2. Cook sausage in the Dutch oven until browned; remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, cook the hash browns until golden brown and crisp.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
  5. Return sausage to the pot, pour egg mixture over sausage and potatoes, and stir gently.
  6. Top everything with shredded cheese, cover, and cook until eggs are set and cheese is melted.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your rugged, hearty breakfast!
