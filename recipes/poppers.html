Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeño Poppers

Description

These bacon-wrapped jalapeño poppers are the perfect appetizer for spice lovers. Creamy, cheesy, and smoky, each bite delivers bold flavor and a satisfying crunch.

They’re easy to make and always a hit at parties, BBQs, or as a rugged snack with a cold drink.

Ingredients

  • 12 fresh jalapeño peppers, halved and seeded
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 12 slices of bacon, cut in half
  • Toothpicks (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Mix cream cheese and cheddar cheese in a bowl until smooth.
  3. Fill each jalapeño half with the cheese mixture.
  4. Wrap each stuffed pepper with a piece of bacon and secure with a toothpick if needed.
  5. Place poppers on a baking sheet lined with foil.
  6. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, or until bacon is crispy.
  7. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.
